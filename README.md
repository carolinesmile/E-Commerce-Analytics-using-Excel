# E-Commerce-Analytics-using-Excel
📊 StellarMart Sales Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project showcases my ability to analyze and visualize retail sales data for StellarMart, covering sales across five product categories (Electronics, Clothing, Home Appliances, Books, and Groceries) in four regions over two years. The dataset includes 5,000 rows of sales data, analyzed to extract actionable insights that drive business decisions.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables & PivotCharts
- VLOOKUP & TEXT formulas

## What I Did
- Cleaned and preprocessed raw sales data
- Fixed typos, inconsistent categories and duplicates
- Added Month-Year and Quarter-Year time columns
- Built PivotTables to analyze sales by category and region
- Created 5 charts to visualize key insights

## Key Findings
- Books is the top performing category (2,733,691 in sales)
- Electronics is the lowest performer (2,623,346)
- Jacket is the best selling product (751,064)
- East and South regions show strongest performance
- All categories are closely competitive within 110,000 range

## Files
- StellarMart_Cleaned.xlsx: A cleaned dataset with analysis, PivotTables, and charts to explore the insights.

## Features of the Dashboard:
- Sales by Category and Region: A Stacked Bar/Column Chart showing the breakdown of sales.
- Customer Segmentation: A Pie Chart visualizing the distribution of customers.
- Top Products: A Horizontal Bar Chart showcasing the best-selling products.
- Sales Trends Over Time: A Line Chart to identify seasonal trends and performance over time.
- Interactive Slicers: Filters to dynamically explore the data by Region, Category, and Month.
